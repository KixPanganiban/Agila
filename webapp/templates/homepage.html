Agila

Agila helps you track and visualize your carbon footprint and energy use. Forms groups, join communities, and help raise awareness about climate change: one device at a time.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

How It Works

Sign up for a free Agila account using your Facebook, register your devices, download the small tracking app, and start tracking immediately. Agila currently supports all desktop platforms and Android. iOS support coming soon.

Free and Open Source

Agila is completely free to use (and reuse). You can even download the source code right now from Github. The anonymized data collected by Agila can also be accessed through the Agila API.
